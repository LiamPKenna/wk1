ABOUT ME

Background:

I was born in a tiny town in Utah and grew up in rural Oregon.

I have had two dogs in my life:

  1. Bear - A collie mutt from age 0 - 8
  2. Bug (Holly) - A yellow lab mutt from age 30 - present

I fell in love with coding while building tools to help me do my previous jobs and have been actively growing my skills ever since.
